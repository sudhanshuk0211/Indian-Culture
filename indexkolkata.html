About

            

            
Kolkata is home to the Victoria Memorial, a large white marble building that was built to commemorate Queen Victoria's reign. It is now a popular tourist attraction. The Howrah Bridge in Kolkata is one of the busiest bridges in the world, with over 100,000 vehicles and pedestrians crossing it every day

            

            

            
Culture

            

            
Kolkata is a culturally rich city with a long history of literature, art, music, food, and festivals. The city is known for its famous writers, poets, and artists, its classical music, street food, and the Durga Puja festival.

            

            

            
Food

            

            
Kolkata is famous for its diverse and flavorful cuisine, which is a blend of Bengali, Mughlai, and British influences. It is famous for its street food, including puchkas, jhal muri, and roshogolla.
